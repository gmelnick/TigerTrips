Princeton is full of hidden gems waiting to be explored. But sometimes those gems are exactly that - hidden! Our goal is to change the way that people in and around Princeton experience and appreciate this wonderful community. You can access the TigerTrips prototype website here.

Jasmine Peled

Jasmine is a senior studying computer science and philosophy at Princeton. She is interested in technology, ethics, privacy, security, public policy, and any intersection of those fields. Despite being a senior, she is constantly finding new things to do in Princeton that she never knew existed and wants to help fellow students and others in the area discover these as well!

Agnieszka Dutkowska-Zuk

Agnieszka is an auditor of the HCI class. She holds a master’s degree in Psychology (M.A.) and in Applied Behavior Analysis (M.Sc.). Before coming to Princeton, she was a therapist for children with autism. Agnieszka is from Warsaw, Poland but since she loves travelling, she also lived in U.K., California and China. She is interested in other cultures, languages and people in general. During her free time Agnieszka enjoys wandering around Princeton, watching a good movie at Princeton Garden Theatre and volunteering in Arts Council of Princeton.
